Recipe for Pastitsio

  • Time: Preparation 40 min _ Bake 40 min
  • Serves: 8-10
Ingredients Quantity


For the ground meat


• Ground pork meat 500 - 700gr
• Chopped tomatoes 1 can / 500gr
• Onions (finely chopped) 2
• Olive oil 6 tablespoons
• Parsley (finely chopped) ½ bunch
• Pepper a pinch




For the béchamel sauce
   
• Milk (fresh or evaporated) 1 litre
• Flour 100 gr
• Olive oil 2 tablespoons
• Olive oilt 4 tablespoons
• Peper a pinch




For the pasta


• Macaroni No2 500gr
  (Alternatively use:
  long ziti, bucatini,
  or penne rigate)


After putting it together


• Breadcrumbs 50gr

Step 1:

Place a saucepan over high heat. Add the olive oil and the finely chopped onions and parsley. Sauté for 3 minutes, until the onions caramelize.

Step 2:

Add the tomatoes and the pepper. Then, add the ground pork meat. Simmer for 20-25 minutes. Remove form fire.

Step 3:

Place another pot over high heat and add water. When the water boils add a pinch of salt and the macaroni No2. Boil for 7-8 minutes (no more) - do not overcook. It needs to be al dente. Drain the pasta.

Step 4:

Put another pan over heat. Add the milk and the flour. (Leave 1/3 of the milk and mix it with some flour in a bowl).
Then, add pepper and whisk continuously so that no lumps form in the mixture.
When the mixture starts to boil, add the rest of the mixture of the milk (1/3) and the flour in the bowl. Continue whisking occasionally, and let it boil over low heat, until the sauce thickens. Remove from fire.

Step 5:

Put the cooked macaroni in a baking pan. Add the tomatoes and the pepper.
Add the cooked ground pork meat mixture and spread over the pasta.
Pour the béchamel sauce over the top and spread evenly.
Sprinkle the breadcrumbs over the béchamel sauce.

Recipe for Pasta with Tomato sauce

  • Time: Preparation 20 min
  • Serves: 4 - 6
Ingredients Quantity
• Linguini 500gr
• Fresh ripe tomatoes 1 kilo
  (Alternatively use whole
  peeled canned tomatoes)		 (2 cans)
• Olive oil 4 tablespoons
• Pepper a pinch
• Salt a pinch




To serve
   
• Olive oil 2-3 spoonfuls
• Fresh basil ½ bunch
• Peper a pinch




Step 1:

Place a saucepan over high heat and add the olive oil, the tomatoes and the pepper. Boil for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.

Step 2:

Place another pot over high heat and when the water boils add a pinch of salt and the linguini. Boil for 10 minutes.

Step 3:

Drain the linguini when it is ready and place it back in the pot. Add the oil, the pepper and the basil. Serve.
